ShipIt Day

How far will you go?

Event description

What it is


ShipIt Day is a 24-hour event in which employees deliver innovation and value to the company they work for. It's called ShipIt Day because you have to deliver in 24 hours.


Within this time, people are not disturbed for regular work. They have total autonomy over the project they are enthusiastic about. They decide for themselves what they will be working on, who they are going to work with, and how they are going to do it. External guests can participate as team members based on nominal invitations.


The teams who sign up present the results to the evaluators at the end of the event. Employees that do not participate will have the option to vote for their favourite idea and the vote will be taken into consideration in the evaluation process.


The most important rule is to get creative, innovative and have fun!

How it works

  • Employees submit projects ideas.
  • Project ideas are published within the organization.
  • Project owners have to find teammates to work on their project.
  • Employees can ask project owners to allow them to be part of the team.
  • After the kick-off, the teams have 24 hours to implement the idea and then present it.
  • The organization provides food, snacks and drinks for teams.
  • After the allocated 24 hours, the ideas are evaluated by a committee and the winner is selected.
